Understanding Your Investments

What is SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds or other investment vehicles. It helps in building wealth through the power of compounding and rupee cost averaging.

What is SWP?

A Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) enables you to withdraw a fixed amount regularly from your accumulated investments. It's ideal for generating regular income from your investment corpus.

Tax Implications

Long-term capital gains from equity mutual funds are taxed at 10% for gains above ₹1 lakh in a financial year. Short-term capital gains are taxed at 15%.
